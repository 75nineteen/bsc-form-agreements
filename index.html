Athlete Code of Conduct

The purpose of this code is to establish consistent expectations. By signing, I agree to the following:

  • I will respect and show courtesy to my teammates and coaches at all times.
  • I will demonstrate good sportsmanship at practices and meets.
  • I will set a good example of behavior and work ethic for younger teammates.
  • I will be respectful of feelings and personal space; inappropriate behavior will result in consequences.
  • I will attend all team meetings and training unless excused by a coach.
  • I will show respect for all facilities and property used during club activities.
